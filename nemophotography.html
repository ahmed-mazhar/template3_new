ABOUT ME

A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Brandi! I'm a wedding and engagement photographer based in Rochester, NY and New York City

I’ve always loved photography, but wedding photography wasn’t actually something I originally had on my radar as a career. My love of people and learning the stories of others led me to study Psychology for my undergrad degree. Right after graduation, I got married and immediately moved to New York City (my second love). While applying to grad schools in NYC, I stumbled upon a wedding photography studio looking for an assistant and thought it would be a great part-time job while I got used to a new city. However, shortly after working there, I connected the dots and realized that photography could be my career. I quickly placed grad school on hold and dove headfirst into the wedding photography world. I’m so happy that my love of photography has turned into what it is today, and that I still get to learn about other people the way I did with Psychology – I’ve been lucky enough to photograph couples in love now for 7 years and couldn’t imagine doing anything else.
